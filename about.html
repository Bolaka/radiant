Radiant – Business analytics using R and Shiny

Radiant is a tool disigned to facilitate decision making using data and models. It is currently used in the Quantitive Analysis and Research for Marketing Decisions classes at the Rady School of Management. Developed by Vincent Nijs and Terrence August. You can reach us at radiant@rady.ucsd.edu.
